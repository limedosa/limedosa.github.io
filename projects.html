Bat Population Predictive Model

Found, standardized, and merged 3 bat population datasets including geolocation, acoustic, timestamp data.

Explored bat population decline in North America with two ML predictive models using binary classification.

Both models explored whether bat populations at a given time had White Nose Syndrome(a disease that wakes bats up from hybernation and kills them).

Applied tokenization techniques to amalgamate the data into a singular sentence vector and constructed a Natural Language Processing (NLP) model with an accuracy of 87%.

Constructed a Random Forest model achieving a 97% accuracy rate in predicting whether bat populations had White Nose Syndrome at a given time.
